Oxford vibe

Formal Hall

Formal halls are formal dinners at Oxford colleges that happen around twice per week. During Michaelmas term, we "had" to go to two formal halls per week. The food is usually very good and the portions are decent (at least at Pembroke College, I've heard horror stories from other colleges). The dress code is formal, so you have to wear a suit or a tuxedo.

Matriculation

This is a formal ceremony where you are officially admitted to the University of Oxford. You wear a sub-fusc (formal academic dress) and a cap. The ceremony is held in the Sheldonian Theatre.

Radcliffe Camera

The Radcliffe Camera is a famous library in Oxford. It is part of the Bodleian Library and is one of the most iconic buildings in Oxford. It is a great place to study and read books.
